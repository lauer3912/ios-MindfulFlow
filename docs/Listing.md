## App Name

## Description
Transform your daily routine with MindfulFlow – your personal meditation and mindfulness companion designed for busy professionals, meditation beginners, and anyone seeking better mental wellness.

### Key Features:
- **Guided Meditation Sessions**: Follow expert-designed meditation routines with clear timer-based guidance
- **Breathing Exercises**: Box breathing, 4-7-8 technique, and custom breathing patterns
- **Ambient Soundscapes**: Rain, forest, ocean waves, white noise, and sound mixer
- **Sleep Aid**: Sleep stories and gentle sounds to help you drift off
- **Mood Tracking**: Track your daily mood and see trends over time
- **Progress Statistics**: Build healthy habits with streak tracking and calendar heatmaps
- **Dark & Light Themes**: Beautiful design optimized for day and night use
